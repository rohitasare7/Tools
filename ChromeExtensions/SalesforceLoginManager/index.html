Privacy Policy for Salesforce Login Manager Chrome Extension

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Salesforce Login Manager Chrome Extension (the "Extension").

Information We Collect

The Salesforce Login Manager Chrome Extension does not collect any personally identifiable information from users. However, it may collect and store the following information locally on your device:

  • Salesforce login credentials (username and password) provided by the user for the purpose of logging in to Salesforce accounts.
  • Settings and preferences chosen by the user within the extension.

Data Encryption

The Salesforce Login Manager Chrome Extension utilizes encryption to secure the storage of usernames and passwords provided by users. This encryption ensures that sensitive information is protected from unauthorized access or disclosure. The encrypted data is stored locally in the Chrome storage and is not accessible to anyone other than the user who provided it.

How We Use Information

The information collected and stored by the Salesforce Login Manager Chrome Extension is solely used for the purpose of facilitating the login process to Salesforce accounts. This information is stored locally on the user's device and is not transmitted or shared with any third parties.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure the security of the information collected and stored by the Salesforce Login Manager Chrome Extension. This includes implementing encryption and other security protocols to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

We encourage users to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Your continued use of the Extension after any modifications to the Privacy Policy will constitute your acknowledgment of the modifications and your consent to abide and be bound by the updated Privacy Policy.
